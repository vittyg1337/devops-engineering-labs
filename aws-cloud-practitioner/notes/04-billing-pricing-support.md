# Billing, Pricing, and Support

## Pricing Model Basics

AWS pricing is primarily usage-based. Costs vary by service type, region, duration, data transfer, storage volume, and performance configuration.

## Cost Estimation

The AWS Pricing Calculator is used to estimate expected spend before resources are deployed. Estimates improve when workload assumptions are specific and realistic.

## Billing Visibility

AWS Billing and Cost Management helps users review charges, invoices, and account-level spend. Cost visibility is necessary for budget control and early detection of unexpected usage.

## Free Tier Awareness

The AWS Free Tier provides limited usage for selected services. It is useful for learning, but Free Tier eligibility does not eliminate the need to review pricing details and monitor ongoing charges.

## Support Plans

AWS offers multiple support plans with different response times and guidance levels. Understanding the role of support plans is part of the exam domain even for foundational learners.
